### Design. Test. Destroy.
Ramshackle is a multiplayer sandbox where engineering meets destruction. Instead of simple block placement, you build your "Vessel" structure from the ground up with point-placed struts and armor plates. Shape your hull, integrate internal modules (engines, transmission, ammo), and manage the tradeoffs between armor, firepower, and mobility.
