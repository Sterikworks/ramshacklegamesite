Ramshackle is a multiplayer sandbox game where you design a combat vehicle, or "Vessel", out of destructible parts and armor, and put it to the test against other player-driven and AI Vessels. Share designs with other players on your server or license it out to the AI and encounter it in battle!

### Early Access
This is our dream game we've been working on for over 5 years. The game has a solid foundation, and we view Early Access as an opportunity to continue building on that core along with the community.

**What's in the game now?**
As of February 2026, the game includes fully functioning drop-in/drop-out gamemodes for singleplayer and multiplayer, a vehicle editor with seamless "test" mode, and basic tutorials.

**What is the future vision?**
We plan to expand with additional modules, weapons, and materials. Our long-term vision includes a campaign that can be played solo or in co-op, as well as dynamic gamemodes on large-scale maps, and Steam Workshop support.
