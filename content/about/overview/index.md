### Design. Test. Destroy.
Ramshackle is a multiplayer sandbox where engineering meets destruction. Build unique "Vessels" using over 200 detailed structural parts, armor, engines, weapons, and modules. Fight in tactical battles where armor angle, internal layout, and shot placement matter. Share your designs with other players or let AI use them in combat.
