Ramshackle is a twist on the vehicular build-and-combat genre. Instead of piecing together blocks, you construct vehicles with point-placed struts and armor plates, then integrate modules that rely on real internal systems.

Steam page: [Ramshackle on Steam](https://store.steampowered.com/app/3800200/Ramshackle/)
